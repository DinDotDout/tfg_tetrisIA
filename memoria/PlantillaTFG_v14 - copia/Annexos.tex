\section{USB to TTl}\label{appendix:USBTOTTL}
A USB-TTL converter mediates between the rather complex signalling that USB uses, and a simple serial bit stream, using TTL voltage levels and asynchronous communication (ASCII). With the proper drivers and a USB-TTL converter installed, a computer can use any sort of “tty” comm protocol or app to talk to an arbitrary serial device (e,g, an Arduino or a GPS unit). %https://www.quora.com/What-is-a-TTL-signal-in-communication-and-when-using-a-USB-port-why-do-we-use-a-USB-to-TTL-converter
